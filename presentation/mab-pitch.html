The bottleneck

Every new government service today is an engineering project.

Tawakkalna hosts 600+ digital services from dozens of government partners. Each new service starts a full development cycle — and that's the wall between citizens and the services they need.

Weeks per mini-app

Even a simple service form requires a full development sprint from brief to production.

Engineering bottleneck

A single engineering team serves every partner, so priority always wins — and everyone else waits.

Inconsistent UX

Every team ships a different look and flow, eroding trust and training citizens to re-learn each service.

Hard to maintain

Dozens of standalone codebases compound into a long tail of tech debt and security surface area.

The answer

MAB — build mini-apps in the browser.

A visual composer for Tawakkalna mini-apps. Partners, analysts, and designers assemble fully-functional experiences on an interactive phone canvas — MAB generates clean, production-grade code automatically.

1

Compose

Drag elements — forms, maps, media, tables — onto an interactive phone canvas. What you see is what you ship.

Configure

Set data sources, styles, languages, and partner theming. Preview changes live on the device canvas.

3

Export

Download a portable package, ready to embed directly into the Tawakkalna super-app. No handoff, no rebuild.
